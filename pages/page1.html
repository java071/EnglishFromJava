Appearance in business communication

The main requirement for the appearance of a business woman is elegant clothes, an attractive hairstyle, discreet makeup and impressive accessories.

At work, the so-called extra-fashionable clothes, bright cosmetics, and an abundance of jewelry are inappropriate. Also, disrespect for colleagues and visitors would be negligence in clothing, slovenliness, provocation.

The main requirement for the appearance of a business man is an elegant suit, a long-sleeved shirt, a well-groomed hairstyle and impressive accessories.

At work, the so-called extra-fashionable clothes, an abundance of jewelry, are too conspicuous. Also, disrespect for colleagues and visitors would be negligence in clothing, slovenliness, provocation.
